Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

TL;DR

Your data stays on your device. QuickFill has no server, no tracking, no analytics, and no remote code. We never see, collect, or share anything you save.

QuickFill is a Chrome extension that lets you save profile information locally and auto-fill forms on websites you choose to use. This page explains exactly how your data is handled.

What QuickFill does

QuickFill helps you save form data — including custom fields — and auto-fill matching inputs on web pages. It can also generate a randomized fake identity for sign-ups you don't want to hand real information to.

Information QuickFill handles

QuickFill only handles information you choose to enter into the extension. That may include:

  • Names, email addresses, and phone numbers
  • Postal addresses and shipping details
  • Travel details such as passport numbers
  • Education, employment, and payment details
  • Health-related fields and any custom fields you create

To match saved profile data to the correct inputs, QuickFill also reads form field labels, names, placeholders, and related page content on the sites you visit.

How information is used

QuickFill uses this information only to provide its autofill features. Saved profile data is used to fill forms when you click the extension or use the autofill controls. That's it. It is not used for anything else.

Where information is stored

QuickFill stores saved profiles and settings locally in your browser, using the Chrome extension storage API on your device. QuickFill does not operate a backend service and does not store your data on any server.

Sharing and selling

QuickFill does not sell your data. QuickFill does not share your saved profile data with the developer or any third party. There are no analytics, no telemetry, and no tracking pixels anywhere in the extension.

Remote code

QuickFill does not use remotely hosted executable code. All executable code is packaged inside the extension and can be inspected in the open source repository.

User control

You control everything you enter into QuickFill. You can edit, replace, or remove saved profile information at any time through the extension popup. Uninstalling the extension removes all saved data from your device.

Open source

QuickFill is fully open source. Anyone can inspect the code, verify these claims, and contribute. The source code is available on GitHub.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the publisher through the contact information listed on the Chrome Web Store listing for QuickFill, or open an issue on the GitHub repository.
